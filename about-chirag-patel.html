Biography

Chirag Patel (CP) is Head of Europe – AI & Digital Transformation at Hidden Brains, a global technology consulting firm, based in Munich, Germany. A Stevie® Award-winning technology leader, he works with enterprises across Europe to adopt AI, modernize legacy systems, and execute digital transformation strategies. He partners with organizations across manufacturing, logistics, retail, and enterprise services to turn AI ambition into execution-ready roadmaps and measurable operational outcomes.

With experience spanning software engineering, product strategy, and business development, Chirag brings a full-stack perspective to technology consulting. He focuses on aligning business objectives with scalable, practical technology solutions — bridging the gap between strategy and execution. He works extensively across the DACH region, BENELUX, and the Nordics, engaging with leadership teams navigating the intersection of strategy, innovation, and execution.

His focus areas include enterprise AI adoption, generative AI strategy, legacy system modernization, data readiness, and end-to-end digital transformation programmes. Chirag is a regular contributor on enterprise AI, digital transformation, and emerging technology trends, and speaks at industry forums across Europe.
