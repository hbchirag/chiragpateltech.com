Chirag Patel (CP) is Head of Europe – AI & Digital Transformation at Hidden Brains, based in Munich, Germany. A Stevie® Award-winning technology leader, he works with enterprises across Europe to adopt AI, modernize legacy systems, and execute digital transformation strategies. He partners with organizations across manufacturing, logistics, retail, and enterprise services to turn AI ambition into execution-ready roadmaps and measurable operational outcomes.

With experience spanning software engineering, product strategy, and business development, Chirag brings a full-stack perspective to technology consulting. He focuses on aligning business objectives with scalable, practical technology solutions. Chirag is a regular contributor on enterprise AI, digital transformation, and emerging technology trends, and speaks at industry forums across Europe.
