Chirag Patel's Role at Hidden Brains

As Head of Europe – AI & Digital Transformation at Hidden Brains, Chirag Patel leads all European market operations and client-facing consulting work. His mandate spans business development, strategic client partnerships, delivery oversight, and thought leadership for the European enterprise technology market.

Hidden Brains is a global technology consulting firm headquartered in Ahmedabad, India, with clients across Europe, North America, and Asia-Pacific. Chirag Patel's role bridges Hidden Brains' global technical capabilities — spanning AI development, software engineering, cloud infrastructure, and data engineering — with the specific operational and regulatory needs of European enterprise clients.

He is responsible for translating Hidden Brains' service portfolio into practical, execution-ready programmes for European organizations, working at the intersection of technology consulting, AI strategy, and business transformation.
